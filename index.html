Our Specials

Chicken Vindaloo

Originating from Goa, Chicken Vindaloo is a spicy and tangy dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a vinegar-based sauce with spices like cumin, coriander, and red chilies.

Kerala Fish Curry

Kerala, on India's southwest coast, is renowned for its spicy and flavorful fish curry, often made with a variety of fish like kingfish or sardines. The curry features coconut milk, curry leaves, and a host of spices.

Dal Makhani

A creamy and indulgent Punjabi dish, dal makhani is made from black lentils and kidney beans cooked with butter and cream. It's a comforting staple often paired with naan or rice.
