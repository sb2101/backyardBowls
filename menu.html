Menu

Breakfast & Starters

Idli
These are steamed rice cakes made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter. They are typically served with coconut chutney and sambar (a spicy lentil soup).
Dosa
A thin, crispy pancake made from a similar batter as idli but spread thin on a griddle. They can be filled with various fillings like masala (potato), cheese, or onions.
Upma
A savory porridge made from semolina (rava) cooked with vegetables and spices. It's often garnished with cashews and served with coconut chutney.
Poha
Flattened rice flakes cooked with onions, peas, and various spices. It's a quick and light breakfast option.

Main Course

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)
A rich and creamy tomato-based curry with tender pieces of marinated chicken. It's typically seasoned with a blend of spices, butter, cream, and served with naan or rice.
Biryani
A fragrant rice dish cooked with basmati rice, aromatic spices, and your choice of protein (chicken, lamb, or vegetables). Biryani is often garnished with fried onions, mint, and served with raita (yogurt-based side dish).
Paneer Tikka Masala
A vegetarian delight featuring paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in spices and yogurt, grilled or roasted, and then simmered in a rich tomato-based gravy.
Rogan Josh
A flavorful Kashmiri curry made with tender pieces of meat (usually lamb or goat) cooked in a red gravy infused with spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.

Dinner

Chicken Curry
A classic Indian dinner option, chicken curry features tender pieces of chicken cooked in a flavorful gravy made from tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. It's typically served with rice or various types of bread like naan or roti.
Dal Tadka
A comforting vegetarian dish made from lentils (usually yellow or red lentils) cooked with spices and then tempered with ghee, cumin seeds, and red chilies. It's often served with rice or roti.
Fish Curry
In coastal regions of India, fish curry is a popular dinner choice. It features fish pieces simmered in a spicy and tangy coconut-based curry sauce, often served with steamed rice.
Palak Paneer
A creamy vegetarian dish made from paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and a spinach-based gravy, often seasoned with spices like cumin and garam masala. It's a nutritious and delicious dinner option, typically served with naan or rice.

Desserts

Gulab Jamun
These deep-fried milk dumplings are soaked in sugar syrup, making them incredibly sweet and delicious. They are often flavored with cardamom and rosewater, and sometimes served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
Rasgulla
Rasgulla is a Bengali dessert made from fresh cottage cheese (chhena) balls soaked in sugar syrup. They are spongy and light, with a subtle sweetness and a hint of cardamom.
Jalebi
Jalebi is a sweet, deep-fried pretzel or spiral-shaped dessert made from a fermented batter of wheat flour and yogurt. After frying, it's soaked in sugar syrup, giving it a bright orange color and a sweet, syrupy taste.
Kheer
Kheer is a creamy Indian rice pudding made by simmering rice in milk and sweetening it with sugar. It's flavored with cardamom, saffron, and garnished with chopped nuts like almonds and pistachios.
